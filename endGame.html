About the Quiz game

This Quiz game was created using Open Trivia Database API.

To start the game choose a category then you will get 10 questions of medium difficulty from your chosen category.

If your selection is the right answer, it will highlight in green, otherwise you will see your selection highlited in red.

At the end, you will be able to see how many questions you got right.

Have fun!
